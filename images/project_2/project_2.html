Wedding Invitation

This project is very special to me since it was the invitation to my older sister's wedding. She gave me her list of what she wanted to see, and I got to work. On her list was of course, her information, their wedding colors which were pastel pink and grey, and roses. Since this was a very formal invitation, I decided to not use numbers for the time and address and instead wrote out the full words which the bride and groom thought was a nice touch. This project was a great experience of being given exactly what the client wants, giving them more and seeing what works for them and what alterations I need to make to create the perfect design for them.
