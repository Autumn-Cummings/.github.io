Phone App Screen Designs

For this project I had to create an original app and make the screen designs for it. I decided to make a chat room app for people specifically where the creators could be involved with their audiences. They could directly communicate with their communities and even let them know events they would attend, when merchandise was released and any announcements they want to release. There are many chat room apps out there but I wanted mine to be one that was run by the creators. There would only be the one verified chat instead of millions of chats created by anyone where false information could be shared.
